Digital Advance is a leading New Zealand online publisher and Internet consultancy formed in March 2008.

Digital Advance’s websites include personal finance site guide2.co.nz, news and views platform Voxy.co.nz, competitions and giveaways database, Gimme.co.nz, consumer recommendations website, Recommended.co.nz and, in Australia, guide and ‘how to’ resources website, guide2.com.au.
